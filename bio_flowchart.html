Critical path & advising notes
  • CHEM chain is the longest bottleneck: CHEM 105 (Y1F) → CHEM 106 (Y1Sp, C− min) → CHEM 209 (Y2F, C− min) → CHEM 210 (Y2Sp). This chain also gates BIO 306 and BIO 310. Missing CHEM 105 in Y1 fall delays Organic Chemistry by a full year.
  • Grade minimums on the CHEM chain: C− required for CHEM 105→106 and CHEM 106→209. No minimum is stated beyond those two transitions, though academic standing requirements apply overall.
  • BIO 306 (Genetics) is the central hub. It requires CHEM 210 (earliest: Y2 spring), so Y3 fall is the earliest it can be taken. It gates BIO 455 (+ senior standing), BIO 470 (capstone), BIO 401 (elective), and HS 412 (elective). Any delay cascades to graduation.
  • BIO 306 is placed in Y3 fall — consistent with the course description (fall only). This gives one full semester of Genetics before BIO 455 and BIO 470 in Y4 spring, and positions BIO 401 cleanly in Y4 fall.
  • BIO 455 requires senior standing in addition to BIO 306. Even if BIO 306 is complete, BIO 455 cannot be taken until Y4. It is spring only — so Y4 spring is the only viable slot in a standard four-year plan.
  • MATH 109 vs MATH 123: Students who place into Precalculus must take MATH 109 first, adding one semester before MATH 123 is available. This delays PHYS 123 by a year if PHYS 123 is the chosen physics option — pushing it to Y4 or requiring the PHYS 110/101 alternative instead.
  • PHYS options matter for pre-med: PHYS 123+124 (calculus-based) is strongly recommended for students intending medical school or graduate programs. Contact the pre-med advisor or department chair for guidance on the full pre-med sequence.
  • HS 315 access for Biology majors: HS 315 (Protein Folding) lists BIO 216 as a prerequisite — a course not required in the Biology major. Biology students who want this elective will need instructor permission.
  • BIO 240 (Research Projects) has a GPA threshold: a minimum 3.30 in-major GPA is required, plus instructor permission. It is not available to all students.
  • Major GPA: 2.0 minimum across all BIO courses (excl. BIO 15X and BIO 19X), and CHEM 105/106/209/210/219/220, and HS 315/320/412 if taken as part of the degree. Total program: 120 cr. Major core: 62–64 cr. Writing Intensive: 6 cr required. No transfer credit for major-level science lab courses taught online.
